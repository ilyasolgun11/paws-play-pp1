About us

Check your furry friend's Eligibility!

Establishing eligibility requirements at Paws Play is essential to guarantee a safe and enjoyable environment for all dogs in our care. These criteria help us assess factors like health, behavior, and socialization, ensuring that each dog is a good fit for our group setting. By collecting this information, we aim to create a secure and harmonious space while prioritizing the well-being of every dog entrusted to us.

What we do best!

Day Care

From the moment they arrive your dog will be in good company. We understand that every dog is different and we take our time to match individuals to the best group based on their size and play style.

  • Individualized dog induction
  • Up to 8 hours off leash fun
  • 1-2-1 expert dog care
  • Enrichment zones & activity
  • Photos of your dog
  • Warm home-time rinse

Training

Your dog enters a tailored training environment, where we assess their unique traits and match them with a fitting training group. By understanding their individual needs and learning style, we provide personalized attention and an ideal setting for effective learning.

  • Individualized dog training
  • 24/7 expert training
  • 1-2-1 expert dog care
  • Enrichment zones & activity
  • Potty training
  • Socialization training

Health & Wellness

Our daycare provide health-related services such as grooming, nail trimming, teeth brushing, and even veterinary care or health check-ups. These services ensure that your dog not only has a great time socializing but also maintains their overall health and hygiene while in daycare.

  • Regular health checks
  • 1-2-1 expert dog care
  • Clean and safe environments
  • Photos of your dog
  • Warm home-time rinse
  • Regular wellness checks

Puppy Training

The first six months of a puppy's life are critical for developing essential life and social skills. During this time, they are highly receptive to learning and socializing, laying the foundation for their future behavior and adaptability.

  • Puppy care & development
  • Individualized puppy induction
  • Tailored expert puppy advice
  • Key socialization
  • Puppy potty training
  • Puppy socialization training
