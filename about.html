Our Story

Our founders, Grace Shelby and Thomas Shelby, established Paws Play in 2001 with a deep love for dogs at its core. They envisioned a haven where dogs could play, socialize, and receive attentive care. Driven by their passion for our furry friends, Paws Play became a trusted dog daycare providing a safe and enjoyable environment for dogs while their owners are at work or away. Grace and Thomas' commitment to ensuring a happy and nurturing space for every dog has made Paws Play a beloved destination for canine companions seeking quality care and fun-filled days.

Our Mission

Paws Play embodies a mission that transcends mere pet care. Our core purpose revolves around ensuring every dog entrusted to our care thrives in an environment that prioritizes their well-being above all else. We are committed to creating a haven where the happiness and contentment of our furry friends reign supreme. At Paws Play, we believe in fostering an atmosphere that resonates with joy-filled tail wags and echoes the laughter of playful moments. Our unwavering dedication extends beyond providing a place for your pets to stay; it's about crafting an experience that nurtures their physical, mental, and emotional health. Every dog that walks through our doors becomes a cherished member of our extended family. With a team passionately devoted to the well-being of every furry guest, we personalize their stay, ensuring each interaction is filled with care, warmth, and personalized attention.
