Our Story

Our founders, Grace Shelby and Thomas Shelby, established Paws Play in 2001 with a deep love for dogs at its core. They envisioned a haven where dogs could play, socialize, and receive attentive care. Driven by their passion for our furry friends, Paws Play became a trusted dog daycare providing a safe and enjoyable environment for dogs while their owners are at work or away. Grace and Thomas' commitment to ensuring a happy and nurturing space for every dog has made Paws Play a beloved destination for canine companions seeking quality care and fun-filled days.

Our Mission

At Paws Play, founded by Grace and Tommy Shelby, our mission is simple: to provide exceptional care, ensuring every dog thrives in a safe, nurturing environment. Join us in cherishing your furry family members, where their well-being is our top priority.
